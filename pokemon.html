Keyboard Controls

Spacebar: Opens the pause screen.
Arrow Keys: Moves the player and navigates menus.
A Key: Use to interact with the NPC to heal your party and to select menu items.

Notes

This is an early build.
Walk around in the grass and wild Bulbasaur will appear.
Face the NPC and press A to have her heal your Pokemon.
